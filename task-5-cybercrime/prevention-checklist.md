Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

Use strong and unique passwords for all online accounts.
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) whenever possible.
Do not share OTP, passwords, or banking details with anyone.
Verify website URLs before entering login credentials.
Avoid clicking suspicious links or downloading unknown attachments.
Be careful while accepting unknown payment requests on UPI apps.
Always verify internship or job emails before responding.
Keep your devices and software updated with the latest security updates.
